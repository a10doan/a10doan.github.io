Looking at the Temp vs. Latitude graph. One noteable trend is that the temperature goes up towards the equator (latitude of 0). And then decreases on either side. This would suggest that it is hotter in the middle of the planet. Possibly due to the fact that the equator receives the most light from the sun. Furthermore, the equator has greater sun exposure throughout the day (longer periods of sunlight).
